Lasagna

Description

This is a classic, meaty lasagna that features layers of pasta, a rich tomato sauce, and a creamy cheese blend. It’s the perfect comfort food for a family dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 box Lasagna Noodles
  • 2 cups Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 jar Marinara Sauce
  • 15 oz Ricotta Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles until they are al dente.
  3. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet and drain the fat.
  4. Spread a layer of sauce in a baking dish, followed by noodles, meat, and cheese.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
