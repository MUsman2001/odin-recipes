Zinger Burger

Description

The Zinger Burger is the ultimate spicy chicken sandwich. It features a thick, juicy chicken breast double-breaded in a secret spicy flour mix, fried until extra crunchy, and served on a toasted sesame bun with cool mayo and crisp lettuce.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breasts (pounded thin)
  • 1 cup All-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons Cornstarch
  • Spices: Salt, Black pepper, Garlic powder, and plenty of Cayenne pepper
  • 1 Egg (beaten with a splash of water)
  • Sesame seed buns
  • Iceberg lettuce and Mayonnaise
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, cornstarch, and all the dry spices.
  2. Dip each chicken breast into the dry flour mix, then into the beaten egg.
  3. Dip the chicken back into the flour mix a second time, pressing firmly to create those "crunchy flakes."
  4. Heat oil to 350°F (175°C) and fry the chicken for 6-8 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Toast the burger buns lightly on a pan.
  6. Spread a generous amount of mayo on the bottom bun, add the lettuce, and place the hot chicken on top.
  7. Close the burger and serve immediately while it's still crunchy!
