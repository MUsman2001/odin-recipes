Pizza

Description

There is nothing quite like a fresh, crispy pizza straight from the oven. This recipe features a golden, chewy crust topped with a zesty tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, and fresh basil for that authentic Italian feel.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves and dried oregano
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, or peppers)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven (and a pizza stone if you have one) to 475°F (245°C).
  2. On a floured surface, stretch or roll out the dough into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Transfer the dough to a piece of parchment paper or a floured baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese and add your chosen toppings.
  6. Brush the edges of the crust with a little olive oil for a golden finish.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is browned and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, top with fresh basil, and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
